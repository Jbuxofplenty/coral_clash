Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Coral Clash. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our game.

Information We Collect

When you use Coral Clash, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information (email address, username)
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Device information and identifiers
  • Usage data and analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Enable multiplayer functionality
  • Save your game progress
  • Improve game features and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Coral Clash uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - for authentication, database, and hosting
  • Google Sign-In - for account authentication

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Coral Clash is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us
